# Change

## ID

`0021-adoption-ux-quick-wins`

## Type

General

## Objective

Implement the high-priority quick wins from the 0020 product validation: adoption self-evidence, context-aware `Next:` in verify, optional-aware status/doctor output, and OpenSpec explicitly marked as optional — without changing the architecture.

## Scope

### In scope

- `adopt` generates its adoption Change's evidence automatically ("Generated by AIEF during adoption"): artifacts created, standards, signals, recommended Skills, guarantees respected. Remaining human actions stay as unchecked tasks (adapt standards, verify, close).
- `verify` prints a context-aware `Next:` after PASS (analyze/prompt/close depending on the active Change) and after FAIL (fix and re-run); honest message when no open Change exists.
- `status`: required checks (README, AGENTS, Changes) keep ✓/!; optional artifacts (Knowledge, Profiles, Navigator, adapters) show `· not present (optional)` instead of warnings.
- `doctor`: OpenSpec reported as `· openspec: not detected (optional)`.

### Out of scope

- New commands, hidden state, workflow changes, auto-closing the adoption Change (humans verify and close).
- Anything from Change 0022 (visible skills).

## Success Criteria

- `doctor → adopt → verify` reads clean and guided for a first-time user.
- The adoption Change never sits with placeholder evidence after a successful adoption.
- Adopt idempotence and additive-only guarantees preserved.
- Full suite passes; `aief verify` passes.
